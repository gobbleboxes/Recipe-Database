Pepper Jack Turkey Breakfast Bowls

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 556

Ingredients

FOR THE TURKEY:

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey 93/7

6 tbsp (100 g) tomato paste

¼ cup (60 g) ketchup

1 tbsp (15 g) dijon mustard

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

½ cup (120 ml) water

Salt and pepper to taste

3 slices (75 g) pepper jack cheese

FOR THE POTATOES O'BRIEN

3 small (500 g) potatoes

2 medium (300 g) bell peppers

½ medium (100 g) onion

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

FOR THE EGGS:

10 eggs

Directions

FOR THE HARD BOILED EGGS

Place 10 eggs in to a sauce pot and cover with water and a splash or two of vinegar.

Bring to a boil and cover with a tight fitting lid. Remove from the heat and set a timer for 12 minutes.

Drain the water and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking. Peel the eggs and cut them in half.

FOR THE POTATOES:

Preheat your oven to 425°F.

Wash and cut your potatoes, peppers, and onion into a small dice. Toss in a bowl with olive oil, salt, and pepper until everything has been coated evenly.

Spread out on a sheet pan or two and bake for 10-12 minutes, remove from the oven and flip. Return to the oven for another 8-10 minutes or until proper browning is achieved.

FOR THE TURKEY:

Heat some olive oil over medium high heat. Add in the turkey and cook until it has browned and any remaining water has been boiled off.

Add in the tomato paste, ketchup, mustard, and water. Stir to incorporate. Allow it to cook for a few minutes so that the flavors can come together.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly between your 5 containers. Add a ½ slices of pepper jack cheese to the top of the turkey mixture.
