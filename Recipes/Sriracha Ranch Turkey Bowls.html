Sriracha Ranch Turkey Bowls

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 429

Ingredients

FOR THE TURKEY:

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey 93/7

2 tbsp (24 g) ranch dressing mix

2 tbsp (30 ml) rice vinegar

2 tbsp (30 ml) sriracha

1 tsp (5 ml) olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

FOR THE RICE:

2½ cups (375 g) cooked rice

FOR THE ZUCCHINI AND ONIONS

2 small (300 g) sweet onions

2 medium (500 g) zucchinis

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

1 tsp (3 g) garlic powder

salt and pepper to taste

Directions

FOR THE RICE:

Cook enough rice to yield 2½ cups of cooked rice. 1 cup of dry rice will yield around 2-3 cups of cooked rice depending on what kind of rice you use.

FOR THE TURKEY:

Heat a bit of olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the turkey and cook until it has browned. Boil off any water that remains in the skillet from the turkey.

Mix the vinegar, ranch mix, and sriracha together. Pour over the turkey and stir to incorporate. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

FOR THE ZUCCHINI AND ONIONS

Peel your oniona and cut into thin slices. Heat some oil over medium high heat in a skillet and add the onions. Cook them until they have begun to caramelize. Season with salt and pepper.

Wash and cut the zucchini into a large dice. Add some oil to the pan and cook for a few minutes until they have begun to soften and develop color. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste.

You can cook the onions and zucchini together if you wish, they are separated in the image to show detail.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways. Add ½ cup of rice to each dish.
