Sweet Potato Turkey Skillet

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 527

Ingredients

3 (750 g) sweet potatoes

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey (93/7)

½ (100 g) sweet onion

2 (250 g) poblano peppers green peppers work fine

1 Tbsp (15 g) garlic minced

1 Tbsp (8 g) cumin

1 Tbsp (8 g) chili powder

1 Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

5 Tbsp (20 g) green onions

½ cup (56 g) shredded cheese

1 ¼ cup (300 g) salsa

Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Wash and cut your sweet potatoes into a large dice.

Start a large pot of water over high heat and bring to a boil. Add in the sweet potatoes and boil until they are easily pierced by a fork. Drain the water once they have finished cooking.

Wash your peppers and cut the pepper and onion into a small dice.

Add 1 Tbsp of olive oil to a skillet over medium high heat. Add the onion, peppers, and 1 Tbsp of minced garlic to the skillet. Cook for 3 minutes.

After the onions and peppers have cooked for a few minutes, add in the ground turkey and cook until it has browned.

Mix in 1 Tbsp chili powder, 1 Tbsp cumin and salt and pepper to taste.

Add in the sweet potatoes and mix.

Store the salsa in a smaller separate container.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide the meat and potato mixture evenly into each of your containers. Top each dish with shredded cheese, 1 Tbsp of green onions, and ¼ cup salsa.
