Superdeluxo Chocolate Spacecake

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 1 hour Servings: Servings: slices calories: 138

Ingredients

1 ½ cups (340 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

7/8 cup (80 g) oat flour ⁣ ¾ cup + 2 tbsp

¼ cup (30 g) tapioca flour cornstarch works too

½ cup (113 g) unsweetened applesauce ⁣

2 scoop (60 g) vanilla casein protein powder

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla ⁣

1 ½ tbsp (10 g) splenda

2 eggs⁣

¼ cup (60 g) chocolate chips ⁣

2 tsp (8 g) melted butter

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F⁣

Sift flours so no clumps remain. Mix the flours together with casein until incorporated. ⁣

In a separate bowl mix the vanilla, splenda, applesauce, yogurt, and eggs. ⁣

Stir the wet ingredients and dry ingredients together in a large bowl.

Spray an 8×8 or loaf pan with oil. Pour mixture in. Spread it evenly. Bang it against the counter to get as much air out as possible. ⁣If you use an 8×8 you may need more chocolate for the topping because of the extra surface area.

Bake for 24 minutes if using an 8×8 or 30-35 minutes if using a loaf pan. Check doneness using when a toothpick inserted comes out clean. ⁣

Allow to cool. Once cooled melt the chocolate chips and butter together. Pour over top and allow to harden. ⁣

This recipe makes 10 slices.
