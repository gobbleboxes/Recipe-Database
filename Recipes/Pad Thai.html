Pad Thai

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 661

Ingredients

10 oz (283 g) rice noodles⁣

2 lbs (908 g) boneless skinless chicken breast

1 lb (454 g) frozen stir fry vegetables ⁣ You can use fresh, the frozen combo is just easier.

1 Tbsp (15 g) garlic ⁣

2 (2) eggs⁣

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil⁣

2 Tbsp (32 g) melted peanut butter⁣

10 tbsp (150 ml) Pad Thai sauce ⁣ I use my grocery store brand sauce from a jar.

Lime⁣

1/3 cup (49 g) crushed peanuts ⁣

3 (10 g) green onions

Directions

Cook the noodles according to the instructions on the package. ⁣

Cut your raw chicken into thin slices of about ⅛-¼” thick. ⁣

Fill a large pot with water, salt it, and bring it to a light boil. ⁣

Add in the chicken and cook until white throughout. It will only take a couple of minutes. Be careful not to over cook it. ⁣

Thaw the vegetables in the microwave and drain any water. ⁣

In a large skillet over medium high heat, add some olive oil and sauté the garlic and vegetables for a few minutes.

Push the vegetables out of the way, add a bit more oil and crack the eggs into the pan and scramble.

Add in chicken, noodles, sauce and peanut butter. Stir to incorporate. ⁣

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide the food evenly between 5 containers. Top with lime wedge, green onion, and peanuts.
