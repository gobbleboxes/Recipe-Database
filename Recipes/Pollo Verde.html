Pollo Verde

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 4 hours

Ingredients

2 lbs (908 g) boneless skinless chicken breast

1 large (300 g) zucchini

1 large (200 g) red pepper

1 medium (200 g) sweet onion

2 Tbsp (30 ml) olive oil

5 (250 g) tomatillos

2 (40 g) jalapeños use serranos if you like it spicy

1/4 bunch (15 g) cilantro

1 (30 ml) lime

4 cloves (20 g) garlic

1 small (100 g) white onion

2 ½ cups (438 g) pinto beans

2 Tbsp (16 g) cotija cheese

Directions

FOR THE CHICKEN:

In a crockpot or instant pot, add 1–2 cups of water. Salt it and then add the chicken. Cook until it is shreddable. Empty most of the liquid and shred the meat.

FOR THE SALSA:

In a pot of boiling water, add the tomatillos, garlic, white onion (halved) and the jalapeños. Boil until the tomatillos have turned army green. Add to a blender with cilantro, lime juice and salt to taste. Blend to desired consistency.

Mix The Salsa into the chicken and stir.

FOR THE VEGETABLES:

Cut your red pepper, onion, and zucchini into a large dice. Sauté with olive oil until soft. .

FOR THE BEANS:

Rinse and drain the beans.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly between 5 dishes. Divide the Cotija beans evenly and sprinkle over the beans.
