Boneless Ribs with Cauliflower Mac & Cheese

second look at

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 35 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 780

Ingredients

FOR THE RIBS:

2 lbs (908 g) country style pork ribs

¼ cup (72 g) BBQ sauce find a vinegar or tomato based sauce

1 tsp (3 ml) salt

1 tsp (2 ml) pepper

1 Tbsp (7 g) cumin

1 Tbsp (7 g) garlic powder

1 Tbsp (7 g) onion powder

1 tsp (3 g) paprika

1 tsp (3 g) chili powder

FOR THE CAULIFLOWER MAC AND CHEESE:

24 oz (681 g) frozen cauliflower

3 Tbsp (42 g) butter

6 Tbsp (36 g) almond flour regular flour is fine

1 ½ cups (360 ml) whole milk

1 ½ cup (168 g) cheddar jack cheese

Salt and pepper to taste

FOR THE COLESLAW:

2 ½ cups (142 g) coleslaw mix

1 Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

¼ cup (56 g) mayonnaise

1 Tbsp (15 ml) lemon juice

1 Tbsp (21 g) honey

Directions

FOR THE RIBS:

Preheat your oven to 375°F.

In a bowl mix together the seasonings. 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 1 Tbsp cumin, 1 Tbsp garlic powder, 1 Tbsp onion powder, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp chili powder.

Rub the seasonings into all sides of the meat. Bake for 30 minutes and check temperature. Bake until it has reached an internal temperature of at least 155°F.

FOR THE CAULIFLOWER MAC AND CHEESE:

Cook the cauliflower in a microwave until it is no longer frozen. Cut into macaroni noodle sized pieces.

In a sauce pan over medium heat, melt the butter and add in the flour to make a roux. Mix thoroughly and cook for 2-3 minutes to brown.

Add in the milk and stir continuously until it is smooth. Bring to a boil and stir until thick.

Add in 1 cup of cheese and stir.

Stir in the cauliflower. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add the remaining ½ cup of cheese to the top after plating each serving!

FOR THE COLESLAW:

In a bowl, mix together ¼ cup mayonnaise, 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, and 1 Tbsp honey.

Pour over 2 ½ cups of coleslaw mix.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ribs and cauliflower mac and cheese evenly 5 ways into each of your containers. Store the coleslaw in a separate, smaller container because you won’t want to microwave it.
