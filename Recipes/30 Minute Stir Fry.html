30 Minute Stir Fry

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 530

Ingredients

FOR THE TURKEY:

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey (93/7)

8 oz (227 g) mushrooms

1 tbsp (15 g) oil

FOR THE VEGETABLES:

1 medium (200 g) sweet onion

½ lb (227 g) green beans

1 medium (150 g) red pepper

1 tbsp (15 g) oil

1 tbsp (15 g) minced ginger

1 tbsp (15 g) minced garlic

2 tsp (10 g) soy sauce

salt and pepper to taste

FOR THE RICE:

3¾ cups (563 g) cooked rice

FOR THE SAUCE:

3 tbsp (45 g) soy sauce

2 tbsp (30 g) gochujang

2 tbsp (30 g) mirin can sub with half the amount of honey

1 tsp (5 g) sesame oil

2 tsp (10 g) rice vinegar

Directions

FOR THE RICE:

Cook enough rice to yield 3¾ cups of cooked rice. 1 cup of dry rice will make 2-3 cups of cooked rice depending on what kind of rice you make.

FOR THE TURKEY AND VEGETABLES

In a large skillet over medium high heat, add some oil and brown the turkey. Season lightly with salt and pepper.

Cut all of your vegetables while the turkey cooks. Cut the mushrooms into a medium dice, the onion and pepper into a large dice, and the green beens into small pieces.

Once the turkey has browned, add in the mushrooms and allow them to cook down.

In another skillet over medium high heat, add some oil and the onions and peppers first. Allow them to develop some color and soften then add in the ginger and green beans. A minute or two later, add in the garlic and stir to combine.

Drizzle 2 tsp of soy sauce over the top of the vegetables and adjust flavor to taste with salt and pepper.

FOR THE SAUCE:

While everything is cooking you can prepare you sauce by combining the soy sauce, gochujang, mirin, rice vinegar, and sesame oil in a bowl and mixing.

Once the mushrooms have cooked down, pour the sauce over the top and mix, allow it to cook and thicken up a bit. While this is happening you can start to plate your rice and vegetables.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Each dish gets ¾ cup of rice and you can divide the vegetables and turkey evenly between the dishes.
