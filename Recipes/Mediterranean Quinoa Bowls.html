Mediterranean Quinoa Bowls

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 540

Ingredients

FOR THE BEEF MIXTURE:

2 lbs (908 g) ground beef 90/10

2 (120 g) Roma tomatoes

2 tbsp (28 g) red wine vinegar

5 tsp (10 g) feta cheese

FOR THE QUINOA:

2½ cups (438 g) cooked quinoa

FOR THE ROASTED VEGETABLES:

1 medium (250 g) zucchini

1 medium (250 g) yellow squash

1 medium (150 g) red bell pepper

2 tsp (4 g) oregano dried

1 tbsp (15 g) olive oil

10 tbsp (150 g) hummus I use storebought, you can make it if you wish

Directions

FOR THE QUINOA:

Measure out enough quinoa to yield 2½ cups of cooked quinoa. 1 cup of dried quinoa will make 2-3 cups of cooked quinoa.

Rinse the quinoa under water until the water runs clear. Do not skip this step as quinoa has a coating called saponin which will result in a bitter, unpleasant flavor if not rinsed off.

Add 1 cup of quinoa and 1 ½ cups of water to a pot. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat to simmer and cover. Cook for 15 minutes, remove from heat to rest for 5 minutes. Uncover and fluff with a fork.

FOR THE ROASTED VEGETABLES:

Preheat your oven to 425°F.

Wash and cut the vegetables into a large dice. Toss the vegetables with 1 of oil, 2 tsp of oregano, and salt and pepper.

Bake for 10 minutes, flip, and bake an additional 6-8 minutes.

FOR THE BEEF:

Heat a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the beef and cook until it has browned. Drain the fat from the pan.

Wash and cut the tomatoes into a small dice. Remove the seeds.

Season the beef with salt and pepper, add in 2 tbsp of red wine vinegar, and the diced tomatoes. Mix and adjust flavors as needed.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Add ½ cup of cooked quinoa to each dish. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways. Add a 2 tbsp serving of hummus in a smaller separate container to each dish and sprinkle a light dusting of feta cheese over the top.
