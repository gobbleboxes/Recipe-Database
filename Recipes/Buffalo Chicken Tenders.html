Buffalo Chicken Tenders

Prep Time: 3 minutes Cook Time: 8 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 221

Ingredients

1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast tenders

2 tbsp buffalo sauce

salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Preheat your air fryer to 400Â°F.

Season the chicken with salt and pepper and add to the air fryer. Cook the tenders for around 4 minutes and then flip and cook an additional 4 minutes.

Toss the chicken in the buffalo sauce.

This recipe makes 2 servings.
