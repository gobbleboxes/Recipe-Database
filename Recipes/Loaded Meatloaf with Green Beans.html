Loaded Meatloaf with Green Beans

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

FOR THE MEAT MIXTURE:

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey (93/7)

¾ cup (60 g) instant oats

2 (2) eggs

1 cup (240 ml) skim milk

½ medium (100 g) sweet onion

1 cup (100 g) shredded carrots

¼ cup (23 g) parmesan cheese grated

1 tsp (2 g) pepper

1 tsp (3 g) salt

1 tsp (3 g) Lawry’s seasoned salt

FOR THE POTATO WEDGES:

5 small (750 g) russet potatoes

2 Tbsp (30 ml) garlic minced

1 tsp (3 g) salt

1 tsp (2 g) pepper

1 tsp (2 g) paprika

1 tsp (2 g) cayenne pepper

1 Tbsp (1 g) dried parsley

2 Tbsp (11 g) parmesan cheese grated

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

FOR THE GREEN BEANS:

1 lb (454 g) green beans fresh

Directions

FOR THE MEATLOAF:

Preheat oven to 350°F

Finely chop the onion (and the carrots if desired).

In a large bowl combine the turkey, ¾ cup oatmeal, 2 lightly beaten eggs, 1 cup of milk, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 1 tsp seasoned salt, ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese, and the chopped onion and carrots. Try not to handle too much as the meat will become tough.

Press the mixture into a large loaf pan and bake for 1 hour or until the center registers a temperature of 160°F.

FOR THE POTATOES:

Wash each of your potatoes thoroughly. Cut each of them in half long ways 3 times so you have 8 wedges for each potato.

You can use the oven at the same time you are cooking the meatloaf. On a cookie sheet, drizzle 1 Tbsp of olive oil over the potatoes.

Sprinkle 1 tsp each of salt, pepper, paprika, cayenne, and 1 Tbsp parsley over the potatoes. Add 2 Tbsp minced garlic and 2 Tbsp of grated parmesan and toss the potatoes to coat.

Bake them for 15 minutes and then flip them to the other side and cook an additional 10-15 minutes. Watch them carefully to ensure they don’t burn.

FOR THE GREEN BEANS:

If you buy the pre-cut green beans in the bag, follow the instructions on the bag.

If you buy regular green beans, cut off the ends and boil them for 10 minutes or until they are soft.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Cut the meatloaf into 5 equal portions and divide the green beans and potatoes evenly.
