Pepper Jack Cheeseburger Bowls

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 532

Ingredients

FOR THE HAMBURGER AND BROCCOLI

1½ lbs (908 g) ground beef (93/7) or turkey

1 lb (454 g) mushrooms

5 Tbsp (70 g) shredded pepper jack cheese

1 lb (454 g) frozen broccoli

1½ Tbsp (23 g) olive oil

10 Tbsp (150 g) ketchup

5 (150 g) dill pickle spears

2 (120 g) Roma tomatoes

Salt and pepper to taste

FOR THE POTATOES:

5 small (750 g) russet potatoes

1½ Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

1 tsp (3 g) cayenne pepper

1 tsp (3 g) garlic powder

1 tbsp (6 g) chili powder

salt and pepper to taste

Directions

FOR THE POTATOES:

Preheat your oven to 450°F.

Wash each of your potatoes thoroughly. Cut them into a medium dice.

In a large bowl, drizzle 1½ Tbsp of olive oil over the potatoes. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, 1 tsp of cayenne pepper, garlic powder and 1 tbsp chili powder. Toss the potatoes to coat and spread onto a sheet pan.

Bake for 10-15 minutes and then flip them to the other side and cook an additional 10-12 minutes or until desired doneness is achieved.

FOR THE BROCCOLI:

Add a sheet pan to the oven while it is preheating to 450°F. Add 1 Tbsp of olive oil to a large bowl with the broccoli and salt and pepper. Lay the broccoli out on the hot sheet pan and roast for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven, turn, and cook an additional 5-8 minutes.

FOR THE HAMBURGER AND MUSHROOMS

Heat a skillet over medium high heat. Add the ground beef and cook. Once it has browned, season with salt and pepper to taste.

Wash and cut your mushrooms into thin slices. If you want these to develop color, cook them in a dry skillet over medium heat and salt them to pull out the water. They will cook down and brown in about 5-8 minutes. You could also just throw them in with the beef if you don't care to cook them separately.

Mix the mushrooms and beef together and stir to incorporate.

FOR THE BOWLS

Cut 2 Roma tomatoes into slices.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your hamburger, potatoes, and broccoli evenly between your 5 containers. Top the hamburger with 1 Tbsp of shredded pepper jack cheese. Add 1 pickle spear and 2 slices of Roma tomatoes. Store 2 Tbsp of ketchup sauce in a smaller, separate container.
