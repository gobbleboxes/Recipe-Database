Vegetable Shake

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 167

Ingredients

1 pint (473 ml) water⁣

2 tbsp (30 ml) lemon juice⁣

1 cup (85 g) carrot⁣⁣s

4 cups (125 g) kale ⁣

2 (365 g) pears ⁣

1 medium (300 g) cucumber ⁣

1 medium (200 g) zucchini

1 inch (15 g) ginger

Directions

Add all of the ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth. ⁣

Store in mason jars and refrigerate or freeze if you’re prepping more than a days worth. ⁣

Notes

I've started to cut all of the vegetables in advance and store them in meal prep containers instead of blending them and storing in the mason jars. This is better for preserving nutrient quality.
