Funfetti Protein Cake Bites

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 18 minutes Servings: Servings: bites calories: 34

Ingredients

3 tbsp (48 g) liquid egg whites (1 egg white)

2 tbsp (15 g) coconut flour⁣

1 scoop (30 g) vanilla casein protein powder

½ tsp (2 g) stevia⁣

3.5 tbsp (50 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

1 tsp (5 g) rainbow sprinkles ⁣

2 ⅓ tbsp (35 ml) water

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. ⁣

Mix the dry ingredients together until incorporated making sure there is no clumping. ⁣

Add in the Greek yogurt, egg whites and water. Stir to form dough. Add in sprinkles. ⁣

Use a cookie scoop to create balls and place on a greased cookie sheet. If you don’t have a scoop make ~1” balls. ⁣Makes 7.

Bake for 18-20 minutes.

Nutrition

Calories: 34kcal | Carbohydrates: 2.8g | Protein: 5g | Fat: 0.4g
