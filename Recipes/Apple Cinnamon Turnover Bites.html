Apple Cinnamon Turnover Bites

Breakfast

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 13 minutes Servings: Servings: bites calories: 41

Ingredients

FOR THE BASE:

¾ cup (60 g) oat flour

½ cup (120 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

3 tbsp (50 g) liquid egg whites

1 (100 g) apple

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla

½ tsp (2 g) baking soda

½ tsp (2 g) stevia

1 tsp (3 g) cinnamon

2 ½ tsp (10 g) coconut sugar

FOR THE ICING:

3 ½ Tbsp (50 g) vanilla Greek yogurt

2 tsp (5 g) vanilla whey protein powder

½ tsp (1 g) stevia ⁣

2 tsp (8 ml) water

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. ⁣

Cut apple into small dice. Heat a skillet over medium heat & spray with oil. Add apples and sprinkle sugar over top. Sauté for a few minutes until soft. Toss in cinnamon. ⁣

Mix the dry dough ingredients together then add in yogurt, vanilla, and eggs. ⁣

Spray a mini muffin tin with oil. Divide the dough in half. Fill 12 wells using half of the total mixture. Use a spoon to form the dough into the well shape as best you can. ⁣

Divide the apple mix between the wells. Add the remaining dough over the top. ⁣

Bake for 12-13 minutes. ⁣

Mix the icing ingredients and drizzle over the top. It’s best to ice them when you eat them and not in advance.
