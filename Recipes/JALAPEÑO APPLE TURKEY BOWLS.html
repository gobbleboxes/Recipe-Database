JALAPEÑO APPLE TURKEY BOWLS

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 533

Ingredients

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey (93/7)

1 medium (120 g) apples⁣

2 (60 g) jalapeño pepper

1 large (300 g) sweet potato

4-5 small (225 g) red potatoes

4-5 small (225 g) yellow potatoes

20 oz (568 g) chopped frozen broccoli ⁣⁣

1/4 cup (60 ml) soy sauce ⁣⁣

2 tbsp (42 g) honey⁣⁣

1 tbsp (15 ml) Worcestershire sauce ⁣⁣

2 tsp (10 g) garlic ⁣⁣ minced

2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil ⁣⁣

1 tsp (1 ml) oregano ⁣⁣

Directions

Preheat your oven to 450°F⁣⁣

Cut the potatoes into a large dice. ⁣⁣

Coat potatoes in 1 tbsp oil and toss with oregano and salt and pepper. Bake for 10 minutes and turn. Cook 10 more minutes or until desired doneness ⁣⁣

Heat oil in a pan over medium high heat. Brown the turkey and remove/boil off any water in the pan. ⁣⁣

Cut the jalapeño and Apple. Make room in the pan to sauté the jalapeño. Cook for 3-4 minutes. ⁣⁣

In a bowl, mix together the soy sauce, honey, Worcestershire, and garlic. Add to the turkey. ⁣⁣

Remove from heat and stir in the apples. ⁣⁣

You can cook the broccoli if you wish. Not necessary though. Divide all of your ingredients evenly 5 ways.
