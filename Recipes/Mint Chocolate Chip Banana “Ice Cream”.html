Mint Chocolate Chip Banana “Ice Cream”

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 209

Ingredients

2 medium (250 g) frozen bananas

2 scoops (60 g) whey protein

½ cup (120 ml) whole milk

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla extract

1 tbsp (15 g) mini chocolate chips

1-2 drops green food coloring

1-2 drops peppermint extract

Directions

Add the frozen bananas to a high powered blender in small pieces.

Slowly pour in the milk and start to churn.

Add in the whey, mint, and coloring.

Fold in chocolate chips.

Nutrition

Calories: 209kcal | Carbohydrates: 26g | Protein: 18.2g | Fat: 3.6g
