Add in the apple and Greek yogurt and mix until completely hydrated.

Cut out two rectangular pieces of parchment paper about 5 inches by 3 inches. Spray lightly with oil.

Divide the dough into two halves and spread out on each piece of parchment.

Preheat your air fryer to 325Â°F on the air fry setting for 15 minutes.

Spray the top of each fritter with oil and add to the air fryer. You could probably bake these but I would guess they will come out more bread like if you do. It may take longer in the oven.

Keep an eye on the fritters and flip them over about half way through or when the top has browned evenly.

FOR THE FROSTING:

Mix the powdered sugar and milk together.

Top each fritter with frosting.
