Chocolate Chip Cookie Bars

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: bars calories: 101

Ingredients

WET INGREDIENTS

1 can (425 g) cannellini beans

¼ cup (60 ml) maple syrup ⁣

1 scoop (32 g) vanilla casein protein powder

¼ cup (58 g) unsweetened applesauce ⁣

DRY INGREDIENTS

¼ cup (20 g) oat flour⁣

½ tsp (2 g) baking soda⁣

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla ⁣

1 (1) egg⁣

2 tbsp (30 ml) water

2 tbsp (30 g) mini chocolate chips ⁣

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F⁣.

Drain and rinse beans. ⁣

In a food processor or a blender mix everything but the chocolate chips until smooth. ⁣

Spray an 8×8 with oil and pour in the batter. Top with or stir in the chocolate chips. ⁣

Bake for 23-25 minutes. ⁣

Let it cool completely and cut into 10 bars.
