Chocolate Chip Cookie Bread

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 17 minutes Servings: Servings: bites calories: 67

Ingredients

DRY INGREDIENTS

14 tbsp (80 g) oat flour ¾ cup + 2 tbsp

½ tsp (2 g) baking soda⁣

¼ tsp (1 g) salt⁣

WET INGREDIENTS

1 small egg⁣

⅔ cup (150 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

1 ½ tsp (5 g) splenda

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla⁣

¼ cup (60 g) chocolate chips

Directions

Preheat oven to 375°F.

Stir together the dry ingredients and the wet ingredients separately.

Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.

Use a cookie scoop to make balls. Place on a greased cookie sheet and bake for 15-17 minutes⁣. Makes 11.

Nutrition

Calories: 67kcal | Carbohydrates: 8.7g | Protein: 2.6g | Fat: 2.4g
