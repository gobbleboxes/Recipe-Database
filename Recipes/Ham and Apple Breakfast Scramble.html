Ham and Apple Breakfast Scramble

Breakfast

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 405

Ingredients

½ lb (227 g) ham steak

6 eggs

¼ cup (60 ml) milk

1 (300 g) large sweet potato

½ (75 g) small onion

1 (125 g) small green pepper

1 (125 g) golden delicious apple

1 (125 g) avocado

2 Tbsp (30 ml) olive oil

Directions

Preheat your oven to 400°F.

Wash and peel your sweet potato. Cut it into a small/medium dice. Drizzle 1 Tbsp olive oil over the top and roast at 400°F for 10 minutes and flip. Cook an additional 5-10 minutes. or until proper browning has been achieved.

Cut the onion and pepper in a small dice. Add some oil to a skillet over medium high heat. Cook until the onions are soft and translucent. Remove from pan.

Scramble 6 eggs with the milk and cook over medium heat until cooked. Set aside.

Cut the ham into a large dice and sauté in skillet until browned.

Cut the apple into a large dice. Don’t cook this as it will cook in the microwave and you want it to maintain texture.

Add all of you ingredients (minus the avocado) into the pan and stir to incorporate.

PLATING

This recipe makes 4 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly between your 4 containers. Top with a few slices of avocado if desired.
