Sloppy Joe Breakfast Bowls

Breakfast

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 480

Ingredients

1 ½ lbs (681 g) Ground Turkey 93/7⁣

1 Tbsp (15 ml) Olive Oil⁣

1 medium (150 g) Green Bell Pepper⁣

½ medium (100 g) Sweet Onion⁣

⅓ cup (75 g) Tomato Paste⁣

1 Tbsp (15 ml) Worcestershire Sauce⁣

1 Tbsp (15 g) Dijon Mustard⁣

3 Tbsp (45 g) Ketchup⁣

1 Tbsp (5 g) Onion Powder⁣

1 tsp (3 g) Red Pepper Flakes⁣

10 Hardboiled Eggs⁣

2 medium (500 g) Sweet Potato

Salt and pepper to taste⁣

Directions

FOR THE POTATOES:

Preheat your oven to 425°F. Cut potatoes into a small/medium dice. Roast with ½ tbsp oil for 20 minutes. Flip half way. ⁣Cook until you get optimal browning.

FOR THE TURKEY:

Cut the pepper and onion into a small dice. Add ½ Tbsp of olive oil in a skillet and add the pepper and onion. Cook until translucent and add the ground turkey. Cook until browned.

Once the turkey has cooked, stir in tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, ketchup, onion powder, red pepper flakes, and salt and pepper. ⁣

FOR THE HARD BOILED EGGS

Place 10 eggs in a pot of water and bring to a boil. Once boiling, turn off the heat and cover for 12 minutes. Rinse with cold water and peel. ⁣

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients eveny 5 ways.
