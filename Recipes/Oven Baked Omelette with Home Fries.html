Oven Baked Omelette with Home Fries

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 447

Ingredients

FOR THE OMELETTE:

8 eggs

1 cup (114 g) diced ham

1 small (100 g) green pepper

1 small (100 g) red pepper

½ (100 g) yellow onion

¾ cup (84 g) shredded cheddar cheese

¼ cup (60 ml) skim milk

1 Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

FOR THE HOME FRIES:

2 large (550 g) russet potatoes

2 Tbsp (30 ml) olive oil

1 tsp (3 g) seasoned salt

½ tsp (1 g) pepper

1 (60 g) Roma tomato optional

1 (150 g) avocado optional

Directions

FOR THE OMELETTE:

Preheat your oven to 400°F.

Wash your peppers and cut them into a small dice. Dice the onion to a similar size.

Heat a skillet over medium high heat and add 1 Tbsp of olive oil. Add in onion and peppers. Sauté until onions are translucent.

Scramble 8 eggs and ¼ cup of milk together. Add in 1 tsp salt and ½ tsp pepper and mix.

Add in the 1 cup of diced ham, the peppers, onions, and ¼ cup of cheese to the eggs. Top the mixture with the remaining ½ cup of cheese.

Pour the egg mixture into a greased 8×8 or 13×9 inch baking pan.

Bake for 20-25 minutes until the eggs have set.

FOR THE HOME FRIES:

Wash and cut your potatoes into a medium dice, about ½ inch pieces.

Spread them out on a sheet pan and drizzle with 1 Tbsp of olive oil. Season with 1 tsp seasoned salt and ½ tsp of pepper.

Bake for 10 minutes and then flip the potatoes. Bake an additional 10 minutes or until they are crispy.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Cut your omelette into 5 sections, split the potatoes evenly 5 ways, and add to each of your 5 containers. Add avocado or tomato the day you eat each meal if you desire.
