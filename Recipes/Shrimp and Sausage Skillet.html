Shrimp and Sausage Skillet

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 522

Ingredients

1 lb (454 g) shrimp buy peeled and deveined, it's worth it

14 oz (397 g) Kielbasa sausage

½ medium (100 g) sweet onion

2 medium (500 g) zucchinis

2 medium (300 g) red bell peppers

2 Tbsp (30 ml) olive oil

4 tsp (12 g) Cajun seasioning

Salt and pepper to taste

2½ cups (350 g) cooked rice

Directions

FOR THE RICE:

Cook enough rice to yield 2½ cups of cooked rice. 1 cup dry will yield around 3 cups of cooked depending on the type of rice you use.

FOR THE SKILLET

Peel and devein all of your shrimp if needed. Pat them dry with a paper towel and season with around 2 tsp of cajun seasoning.

Heat a skillet over medium high heat and add 1 Tbsp of olive oil. Add the shrimp and cook until both sides are pink. Remove from pan.

Cut the onion and pepper into a large dice. Add 1 Tbsp of olive oil to the skillet and cook the onion and pepper become soft. Remove from pan and set aside.

Cut the zucchini into a large dice and the kielbasa into discs. Add to the skillet and cook until the kielbasa begins to brown.

Combine all of your ingredients into one skillet. Season with cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste. The kielbasa sausage will provide a lot of flavor to this dish on its own.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways into each of your containers and add ½ cup of rice to each.
