Turkey Bulgogi

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 486

Ingredients

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey (93/7)

2 tbsp (30 ml) soy sauce

1 tbsp (15 g) sriracha

1 tbsp (15 g) garlic

1 tbsp (8 g) ginger

½ medium (100 g) onion

1 (150 g) asian pear any pear is fine if you can't find an Asian one

2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil

2 ½ cups (438 g) cooked rice

2 (500 g) zucchini

2 ½ cups (250 g) shredded carrots

Directions

FOR THE RICE:

Cook enough rice to have at least 2 ½ cups of cooked rice. 1 cup of dry rice will yield about 3 cups of cooked rice.

FOR THE TURKEY:

Dice the onion and cut the ginger and garlic into a small mince.

Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a skillet over medium high heat, add the onion, garlic, and ginger. Cook until the onions are soft.

Add the turkey to the skillet and brown.

While the turkey is cooking, grate the pear into a mush. You could use a blender or a food processor here.

Once the turkey has finished cooking, add in the pear, soy sauce, sriracha, and season with salt and pepper to taste.

FOR THE VEGETABLES:

Cut the zucchini into a medium dice.

Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the zucchini and shredded carrots and cook until soft. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly between your 5 containers.
