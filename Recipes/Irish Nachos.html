Irish Nachos

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 8 minutes Servings: Serving calories: 291

Ingredients

1 medium (200 g) russet potato ⁣

2 tbsp (30 g) salsa ⁣

1 tbsp (15 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt ⁣

1 tsp (2 g) green onion⁣

1 tbsp (14 g) cheese⁣

(1 g) oil spray ⁣

Directions

FOR THE POTATOES:

Preheat your air fryer to 400°F on the air fryer setting. ⁣

Wash and slice your potato into chip sized slices.

Add the potatoes to the air fryer and spray lightly with oil. Fry for a few minutes then shake/turn. It will probably take 5-6 minutes depending on the thickness of your potato. ⁣

Remove from the fryer and salt. Sprinkle the cheese over the top and then microwave to melt. ⁣

This recipe makes 1 serving. Top with salsa, yogurt and green onions.
