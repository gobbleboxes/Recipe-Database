Lower Calorie Apple Crisp

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 55 minutes

Ingredients

4 (400 g) apples⁣

¼ cup (30 g) tapioca flour (I used tapioca, anything should work)⁣

2 tsp (8 g) cinnamon⁣

5 tbsp (75 g) maple syrup⁣

1 cup (15 g) puffed millet⁣

½ cup (40 g) oats

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F⁣

Cut apples into slices or chunks. Cut the skin off if you wish, it doesn’t make a difference.

In a large bowl, add the apples, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1 tbsp maple syrup, and 3 tbsp of the flour. Mix thoroughly. ⁣

Pour apples into a greased 8×8 pan. ⁣

In the bowl, mix the oats, millet, remaining 1 tbsp of flour, and 4 tbsp of maple syrup. Mix and pour over apples. ⁣

Bake for 55 minutes or until you can easily insert a knife into the apples.

Nutrition

Calories: 236kcal | Carbohydrates: 55.6g | Protein: 2.1g | Fat: 1.1g
