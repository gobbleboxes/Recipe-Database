Lemon Garlic Pasta

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 686

Ingredients

2 lb (908 g) ground turkey 93/7

1 lb (454 g) brown rice fusilli pasta any kind of pasta works

1 lb (454 g) frozen peas

¼ cup (60 ml) lemon juice

½ medium (100 g) onion

1 tbsp (14 g) butter

6 cloves (30 g) garlic

14.5 oz (411 g) petite diced tomatos 1 can

1 bunch (50 g) lacinato kale regular kale or spinach is fine

¾ cup (80 g) parmesan cheese shredded

2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil

½ cup (120 ml) pasta water

Directions

FOR THE PASTA:

Cook the noodles per the instructions on the package and reserve at least ½ cup of the pasta water for the sauce.

In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium high heat and brown the turkey.

While the turkey is cooking, cut the onion and garlic into a small dice and squeeze the juice out of the lemons.

In a large pot, saute the onions in a bit of olive oil over medium heat until they are soft. Add in the butter and garlic and cook until fragrant.

Stir in 2 tbsp of lemon juice and, peas, and canned tomatoes. Allow the flavors to cook and come together for a couple of minutes.

Add in the noodles, turkey, kale, parmesan cheese, and ½ cup of pasta water. Stir and season with salt, pepper, and the remaining lemon juice to taste.

PLATING

This recipe makes 6 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 6 ways.
