Dijon Balsamic Turkey with Garlic Parmesan Red Potatoes

Prep Time: 25 minutes Cook Time: 35 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 555

Ingredients

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey (93/7)

1.5 lbs (681 g) red potatoes ⁣

2 medium (500 g) zucchini

2 small (300 g) red peppers

1/4 cup (60 ml) balsamic vinegar ⁣

1.5 tbsp (32 g) honey ⁣

1.5 tbsp (22.5 g) Dijon mustard ⁣

2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil ⁣

1/3 cup (56 g) grated Parmesan cheese⁣

2 tbsp (30 g) garlic ⁣ minced

1/2 (100 g) sweet onion ⁣

Salt and pepper ⁣

Directions

Preheat oven to 425F. ⁣

Wash and cut potatoes into 1” pieces. Add to a large bowl with 1 tbsp oil, salt and pepper, 1 tbsp garlic and ⅓ cup grated Parmesan. Toss to coat. ⁣

Add potatoes to a greased sheet pan and bake for 15 minutes, flip and cook an additional 10. ⁣

While the potatoes are cooking, dice the onion and sauté with the garlic a few minutes. ⁣

Add the turkey and brown. ⁣

Mix together the honey, balsamic, and mustard. Add to the turkey once all the water from the meat has boiled off. Season with salt and pepper to taste. ⁣

Cut the zucchini and peppers into 1 inch chunks. Sauté in oil until soft. Season to taste. ⁣

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways. Top with balsamic glaze if you wish.
