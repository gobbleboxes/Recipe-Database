Chocolate Chip Puffed Rice Cookies

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Servings: Servings: cookies calories: 49

Ingredients

DRY INGREDIENTS

2 cups (34 g) puffed rice cereal

6 tbsp (40 g) oat flour

1 tsp (4 g) baking soda

¼ tsp (1 g) salt

WET INGREDIENTS

1 egg

½ cup (113 g) unsweetened applesauce

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla

2 ½ tsp (10 g) stevia

2 tbsp (28 g) mini chocolate chips

Directions

Preheat oven to 325°F. Mix dry ingredients and wet ingredients separately. Combine into one bowl with half the chocolate chips.

Allow the “dough” to rest and hydrate for a few minutes before scooping.

Grease a sheet pan. Using a cookie scoop place the dough onto the pan. Form into cookie shape, top with remaining chocolate chips. I yielded 11 cookies.

Bake 10 minutes.
