Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Protein Muffins

Breakfast

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 22 minutes Servings: Servings: muffins calories: 103

Ingredients

1 cup (80 g) oats

⅔ cup (150 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

2 medium (250 g) total ripe bananas

1 scoop (32 g) whey

1 egg

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla

1 tsp (4 g) baking powder

1 tsp (4 g) stevia

3 tbsp (45 g) dark chocolate chips

Directions

Preheat your oven to 350°F.

In a blender, add the oats and pulverize into a flour. Add in the remainder of the ingredients minus the chocolate chips and blend until smooth.

Spray a muffin tin with cooking spray and fill the wells half way with the batter. It will make 10 Muffins.

Top each with some dark chocolate chips

Bake for 20-22 minutes. I think the muffins are better when they sit overnight
