Gluten Free Cinnamon Sugar Bagels

Breakfast

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: Servings: bagels calories: 77

Ingredients

½ cup (50 g) oat flour ⁣

2 tbsp (15 g) tapioca flour⁣

6 tbsp (80 g) liquid egg whites

½ cup (120 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

½ tsp (2 g) baking soda⁣

2 packets (4 g) stevia

1 ½ tsp (3 g) cinnamon ⁣

2 ½ tsp (10 g) sugar

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. ⁣

Mix all ingredients in a bowl except cinnamon and sugar. ⁣

Fill a donut tin with mixture. Makes 5. Mix half of the sugar and cinnamon together and sprinkle over the top. ⁣

Bake 13-15 minutes. ⁣

Sprinkle the remaining sugar over the top. ⁣

Makes 5. Nutrition is for each bagel. Wait for it to cool before eating. It will be better.
