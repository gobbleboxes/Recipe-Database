Grilled Chicken Sandwiches

Prep Time: 35 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: 5 sandwiches

Ingredients

20 oz (568 g) boneless skinless chicken breast cut into 5 pieces

4 tsp (20 g) Dijon mustard ⁣

2 tsp (10 ml) soy sauce ⁣

1 tsp (3 ml) garlic powder ⁣

2-3 tbsp (30 ml) lemon juice ⁣

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil⁣

⁣FOR THE REMOULADE

3 tbsp (45 g) mayonnaise

2 tbsp (30 g) dill relish ⁣

Lemon juice to taste ⁣

⁣

5 hamburger buns

1 large (125 g) tomato⁣

20 oz (568 g) boneless skinless chicken thighs 5 thighs of roughly 4oz each

4 tsp (20 g) Dijon mustard ⁣

2 tsp (10 ml) soy sauce ⁣

1 tsp (3 ml) garlic powder ⁣

2-3 tbsp (30 ml) lemon juice ⁣

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil⁣

⁣FOR THE SALAD

3 oz (85 g) arugula ⁣

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil⁣

1 tbsp (21 g) honey ⁣

Lemon juice to taste ⁣

1/4 cup (40 g) feta cheese ⁣

Salt ⁣

Directions

FOR THE CHICKEN:

Pound the chicken breast so it is of even thickness throughout. You want it to be around ½ inch thick for optimal cooking. Cut into 5 pieces that are roughly bun sized.

In a bowl mix together the chicken marinade. Stir and adjust flavor as preferred. Add chicken & marinade at room temp for 30 minutes. Start your grill.⁣

Grill the chicken for 3-4 minutes on each side or until cooked through.

FOR THE CHICKEN:

In a bowl mix together the chicken marinade. Stir and adjust flavor as preferred. Add chicken & marinade at room temp for 30 minutes. Start your grill.⁣

Grill the chicken for 3-4 minutes on each side or until cooked through.

FOR THE SALAD:

Mix together the arugula, oil, lemon, honey and feta. Adjust flavor as needed. ⁣

FOR THE REMOULADE:

Mix together the mayo, relish, and lemon juice. Pepper to taste. ⁣

PLATING

Cut the tomato⁣.

Toast the waffles/buns. ⁣

Spread a layer of the mayo on each bun. Top with chicken, tomato, and salad mix. ⁣

Makes 5 sandwiches, nutritional info is for each one.⁣
