Air Fryer Garlic Parm Fries

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 16 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 224

Ingredients

2 medium (450 g) potatoes

1 tsp (3 g) salt

1 tsp (2 g) pepper

1 tsp (2 g) paprika

4 cloves (20 g) garlic

1 tbsp (10 g) grated parmesan cheese

(2 g) cooking spray

1 tbsp (5 g) chopped parsley optional, for garnish

Directions

Wash and cut your potatoes into a thin fry shaped. Around ¼ inch wide.

Peel your garlic and mince using a garlic press.

Finely chop the parsley.

Preheat your air fryer on the air fry setting to 385°F and the timer for 16 minutes.

In a large bowl, toss the potatoes in the salt, pepper, paprika, and spray lightly with oil.

Once the air fryer has heated, add the potatoes and begin the cooking.

After 5-6 minutes of cooking, add the garlic and parmesan and shake the basket. Shake the basket every 2-3 minutes for the remainder of the cooking time until you get your desired consistency.

Garnish with parsley.
