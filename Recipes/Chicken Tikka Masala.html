Chicken Tikka Masala

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 497

Ingredients

2 lbs (908 g) boneless skinless chicken thighs

16 oz (454 g) frozen broccoli ⁣

6 oz (170 g) frozen peas⁣

8 oz (227 g) shredded carrots⁣

2.5 cups (375 g) cooked rice ⁣

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil⁣

Parsley

Directions

Cook enough rice to have 2 ½ cups worth. ⁣

Heat ½ tbsp of oil in a cast iron grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken. Cut into 1 inch pieces. ⁣

In a skillet, heat ½ tbsp of oil over medium heat and add the peas and carrots. Cook for a few minutes. ⁣

Add the chicken and sauce. Stir to incorporate. ⁣

Cook the broccoli in the microwave. ⁣

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly between 5 containers. Put ½ cup of rice in each dish. ⁣
