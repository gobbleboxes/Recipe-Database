5 Minute Cinnamon Roll Center

Prep Time: 3 minutes Cook Time: 2 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 221

Ingredients

2 ½ tbsp (12 g) oat flour ⁣

2 tbsp (10 g) powdered peanut butter⁣

2 tbsp (10 g) tapioca flour ⁣

2 ⅓ tbsp (35 ml) water ⁣

2 tbsp (25 g) liquid egg whites

2 ½ tbsp (12 g) vanilla casein protein powder

1 tsp (4 g) sugar⁣

1 tsp (2 g) cinnamon ⁣

1 tsp (3 g) butter ⁣⁣

Directions

Mix the oat flour, tapioca, PB, 1g cinnamon and casein together. Add in egg whites and water. It will make a dough like consistency. ⁣

Spread out the dough in your bowl to even thickness. ⁣

Melt butter, stir in 1g cinnamon, and sugar. Stir. If it’s too thick, add water to thin it out but no more than a few drops at a time. ⁣

Pour the cinnamon sugar over the top. Microwave for 90-120 seconds
