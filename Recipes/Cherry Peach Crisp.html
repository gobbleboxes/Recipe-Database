Cherry Peach Crisp

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 249

Ingredients

2 (200 g) peaches ⁣

1/4 lb (115 g) sweet cherries⁣

3 tbsp (63 g) honey⁣

1 tsp (5 ml) lemon juice ⁣

1 tbsp (7.5 g) tapioca flour or cornstarch⁣

11 tbsp (55 g) oats ½ cup + 3 tbsp

1/4 cup (25 g) oat flour ⁣

2 tbsp (30 g) coconut oil ⁣

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla ⁣

2 tbsp (10 g) fat free whipped cream ⁣

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. ⁣

Wash and cut your peaches into slices. No need to peel. Pit the cherries and cut in half. ⁣

In a large bowl add the peaches, cherries, tapioca flour, 1 tbsp honey, and lemon juice. Mix and allow to macerate while you make the topping. ⁣

In a separate bowl mix the oats, oat flour, melted coconut oil, 2 tbsp of honey, and vanilla. ⁣

Add the fruit to an 8" cast iron skillet. Top with the oat mixture. ⁣

Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the top has browned. ⁣

Top with whipped cream if you wish.
