Baked Peach Oatmeal

Breakfast

Prep Time: 8 minutes Cook Time: 35 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 347

Ingredients

2 cups (160 g) oats

1 ½ cups (360 g) skim milk

1 Tbsp (21 g) honey

2 scoops (60 g) whey protein powder vanilla

2 cups (280 g) frozen peaches

2 Tbsp (28 g) butter or coconut oil

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla

1 (1) egg

1 tsp (4 g) baking powder

½ tsp (1 g) salt

Cooking spray

Directions

Preheat your oven to 375°F.

In a large bowl, mix together 2 cups of oats, 2 scoops of whey protein powder, 1 tsp baking powder, and ½ tsp of salt.

Spray an 8”x8” baking dish with cooking spray and add the dry ingredients. Pour the wet ingredients over the top and mix to incorporate.

Add in some of the peaches and hide them in the oatmeal. Place some of the peaches over the top for presentation purposes.

Bake the oatmeal for 35 minutes or until it has set in the middle.

This recipe makes 4 servings. Cut the oatmeal into 4 even pieces and place into each of your containers.
