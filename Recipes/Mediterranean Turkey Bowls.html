Mediterranean Turkey Bowls

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 490

Ingredients

FOR THE TURKEY:

2 lbs (908 g) ground turkey 93/7

1½ tbsp (22 ml) olive oil

3 tbsp (45 ml) red wine vinegar

½ cup (100 g) black olives canned

4 (240 g) Roma tomatoes seeded

1¼ cups (100 g) spinach chopped, frozen

1 tsp (3 g) onion powder

1 tsp (3 g) garlic powder

FOR THE RICE:

2 ½ cups (375 g) cooked rice

1 tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

2 tbsp (30 ml) lemon juice

FOR THE TZATZIKI SAUCE:

10 tbsp (300 g) Tzatziki sauce I use storebought, you can make it if needed

Directions

FOR THE TURKEY:

Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a skillet over medium high heat. Add in the turkey and brown.

While the turkey is cooking, cut the tomatoes into a medium dice. Cut out the seeds. Cut the olives as well if you bought whole ones.

Once the turkey has browned, add in the tomatoes, olives, ½ tbsp of olive oil, red wine vinegar, chopped spinach, onion powder, and garlic powder. I use frozen, chopped spinach because it's easier but fresh spinach is fine. Just make sure you allow it to wilt a bit in the pan so you aren't left with huge leaves.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

FOR THE RICE:

Cook enough rice to have 2½ cups of cooked rice. 1 cup of dry rice will make between 2-3 cups depending on which kind you choose.

Once the rice has finished cooking, measure out 3 cups of cooked rice and add in 1 tbsp of olive oil and 2 tbsp of lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

FOR THE TZATZIKI SAUCE:

I use a storebought Tzatziki sauce for ease but you can make it if you need to. There is a recipe included with the Chicken Souvlaki recipe linked here https://mealprepmanual.com/chicken-souvlaki-with-roasted-vegetables/

PLATING

This recipe makes ]5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways and add 2 tbsp of Tzatziki sauce to each.
