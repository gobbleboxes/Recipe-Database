Cajun Chicken with Lentils and Potatoes

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 45 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 510

Ingredients

1¼ lbs (568 g) chicken breast⁣

½ lb (227 g) red lentils dry

2 large (227 g) carrots

2 stalks (114 g) celery

½ medium (100 g) onion

½ tbsp (8 g) minced garlic ⁣

2½ tbsp (37 g) olive oil⁣

½ cup (120 ml) tomato sauce ⁣

2 cups (480 ml) chicken broth⁣

1½ tbsp (11 g) Cajun seasoning⁣

1½ lbs (568 g) russet potatoes ⁣

2 large (500 g) zucchini

Salt and pepper ⁣

5 tbsp (10 g) Parsley⁣ optional

Directions

Preheat your oven to 425°F. ⁣

Cut your onion, celery, and carrots into a small dice. Cut your potatoes and zucchini into a medium dice. ⁣

Rinse your lentils and sift through them to pick out any debris. ⁣

Add 1 tbsp of oil to a large pot and sauté the carrots, onion, celery, and garlic until they have become fragrant and softened. ⁣

Add the lentils, chicken broth, tomato sauce and chicken breast. Bring to a boil and reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 25 minutes. ⁣

While the chicken is cooking, toss the potatoes in 1 tbsp of oil and 1 tbsp of Cajun seasoning. Bake for 12 minutes then turn and bake an additional 12-15 minutes or until you achieve optimal browning. Season to taste. ⁣

Heat the remaining oil in a large skillet and sauté the zucchini until soft. Season to taste. ⁣

Once the chicken is done, remove it from the pot and shred with two forks. Return to the pot and stir. ⁣

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways. ⁣
