Honey Sambal Chicken

second look at

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 455

Ingredients

2.5 lbs (1135 g) boneless skinless chicken thighs

1 ½ cups (225 g) cooked rice ⁣

1 lb (454 g) green beans ⁣

½ medium (100 g) onion

2 cups (300 g) frozen mixed vegetables ⁣

2 (2) eggs⁣

4 tbsp (60 ml) soy sauce ⁣

3 tbsp (45 ml) Sambal olek⁣

2 tbsp (42 g) honey ⁣

1/4 cup (60 ml) water ⁣

1 tbsp (8 g) cornstarch

1 tbsp (15 ml) water

2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil⁣

Directions

FOR THE RICE:

Cook enough rice to yield 1 ½ cups. It’s best if you have day old rice but fresh rice will work.

Dice ½ an onion and sauté in a pan with ½ tbsp olive oil until soft. Add in thawed frozen vegetables. Crack the eggs in and scramble. Stir in rice. Add 2 tbsp soy sauce and season with pepper to taste. ⁣

FOR THE CHICKEN:

Heat a skillet/grill pan over medium heat and cook the chicken. Cut into 1 inch chunks and add back to pan to brown. ⁣

In a sauce pan add 2 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp honey, 3 tbsp Sambal and ¼ cup water. Bring to a light boil. ⁣

Make a slurry with the cornstarch and 1 tbsp of cold water. Reduce heat and stir into sauce to thicken. ⁣

Coat meat with sauce. ⁣

If you use raw green beans, boil or cook them as preferred. ⁣

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide ingredients into 5 servings.
