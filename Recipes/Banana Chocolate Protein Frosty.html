Banana Chocolate Protein Frosty

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 0 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 195

Ingredients

2 medium (250 g) frozen bananas⁣

2 scoops (60 g) whey protein

1/2 cup (120 ml) whole milk⁣

2 tbsp (10 g) cocoa powder ⁣

Directions

Put the frozen bananas in the blender and start churning. ⁣

Add the milk slowly until you reach your desired consistency. If your bananas are bigger or smaller you may need more or less liquid. ⁣

Add the protein and cocoa powders and mix until incorporated.⁣

Nutrition
