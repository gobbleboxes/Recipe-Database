Maple Peanut Butter Doughnuts

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 9 minutes Servings: Servings: doughnuts calories: 137

Ingredients

4/5 cup (80 g) oat flour ⁣

1/4 cup (57 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

1 tsp (4 g) baking powder ⁣

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla ⁣

1 tsp (4 g) stevia ⁣

2 (2) eggs⁣

2 tbsp (30 g) maple syrup ⁣

1 tbsp (16 g) peanut butter⁣

Directions

Preheat your oven to 375F. ⁣⁣

Mix the oat flour, baking powder, and stevia together to remove any clumps. ⁣⁣

Add in the eggs, yogurt, and vanilla. Mix thoroughly ⁣⁣

Filled a greased doughnut tin with the batter. It will make 5. ⁣⁣

Bake 9 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes away clear. ⁣⁣

Mix together the peanut butter and syrup until smooth. ⁣

Once the doughnuts have cooled, top each with the icing.

Nutrition

Calories: 137kcal | Carbohydrates: 17.8g | Protein: 6.4g | Fat: 4.5g
