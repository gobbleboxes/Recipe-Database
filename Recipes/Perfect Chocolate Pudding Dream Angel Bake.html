Perfect Chocolate Pudding Dream Angel Bake

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 22 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 511

Ingredients

9 tbsp (138 g) liquid egg whites ½ cup + 1 tbsp

½ cup (113 g) unsweetened applesauce

1 scoop (30 g) casein protein

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla

½ tsp (2 g) stevia

½ tsp (2 g) baking powder

2 tbsp (30 g) mini chocolate chips

2 tbsp (10 g) cocoa powder

1 pack (92 g) chocolate pudding the snack packs

Directions

Preheat oven to 325°F.

Beat the egg whites, vanilla, & baking powder until stiff peaks form.

Add stevia & cocoa powder.

Add in applesauce & casein. You’ll lose a lot of the volume here, that’s okay.

Pour into a greased 8×8. Top with chocolate chips. Swirl in the pudding.

Bake 20-22 minutes.
