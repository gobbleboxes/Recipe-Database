Frango Assado

Prep Time: 1 hour Cook Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

FOR THE CHICKEN:

10 (1135 g) chicken legs ⁣ 2.5 lbs

1 (1) lime

2 tsp (6 g) chili powder ⁣

2 tsp (2 g) oregano ⁣

2 tsp (6 g) cumin ⁣

1 tsp (3 g) paprika ⁣

1 tsp (3 g) salt ⁣

FOR THE BEANS:

2 cans (795 g) pinto beans

½ medium (100 g) onion⁣

1 tbsp (15 g) garlic ⁣

½ tbsp (7 ml) olive oil⁣

⅔ cup (160 ml) chicken broth⁣

2 tsp (2 g) basil⁣

2 tsp (6 g) paprika ⁣

Salt and pepper to taste ⁣

FOR THE RICE:

1 cup (180 g) dry rice ⁣

½ (100 g) onion⁣

1 tbsp (15 g) garlic ⁣

½ tbsp (7 ml) olive oil⁣

2 cups (480 ml) water ⁣

Salt and pepper to taste ⁣

Directions

FOR THE CHICKEN:

Mix all of the chicken spices and juice from the lime in a ziplock bag. Add the chicken. Marinate at least 1 hour. ⁣

Bake at 400°F for 20-22 min.

FOR THE RICE:

Dice onion and mince garlic. ⁣

Add oil to a pan and add ½ onion and 1 tbsp of garlic. Cook until onion is translucent. ⁣

Add dry rice and allow to brown a bit. Add water, bring to a boil and reduce to simmer. Cover for 20 minutes. Salt and pepper to taste. ⁣

FOR THE BEANS:

In another pan add oil and sauté other half of onion and 1 tbsp of garlic until translucent. Add in ¼ of beans and mash.⁣

Pour in broth and remaining beans. Allow them to thicken and add the seasonings. ⁣

FOR THE FAROFA

Add oil to a skillet and mix in cornmeal. Cook until it has toasted. Season with salt and pepper. ⁣

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide ingredients evenly 5 ways.
