Perfect Chocolate Cream Dream Angel Bake

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 23 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 172

Ingredients

9 tbsp (138 g) liquid egg whites ½ cup + 1 tbsp

½ cup (113 g) unsweetened applesauce ⁣

1 ½ scoops (45 g) vanilla casein protein powder

1 tsp (4 ml) vanilla ⁣

2 tsp (8 g) stevia⁣

¼ tsp (1 g) baking powder⁣

2 tbsp (30 g) mini chocolate chips ⁣

3 tbsp (15 g) cocoa powder⁣

½ cup (113 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

½ cup (113 g) fat free cream cheese ⁣

1 tsp (5 g) oat flour

Directions

Preheat oven to 300°F⁣

Beat the egg whites, vanilla, & baking powder until stiff peaks form. ⁣

Add ½ tsp stevia & cocoa powder. ⁣

Add in applesauce & casein. You’ll lose a lot of the volume here. ⁣

Pour ¾ of it into a greased 8×8. top with chocolate chips. ⁣

Melt cream cheese in microwave. Stir in 1 ½ stevia, yogurt, and oat flour.⁣

Spread a layer of the cream cheese over the brownie. Add the remaining brownie to the top by the spoonful and marble with a knife. ⁣

Bake for 20-23 minutes. Let cool before eating if you want it to be solid.⁣
