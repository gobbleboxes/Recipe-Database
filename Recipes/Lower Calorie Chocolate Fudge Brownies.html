Lower Calorie Chocolate Fudge Brownies

Prep Time: 7 minutes Cook Time: 12 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 234

Ingredients

⅔ cup (75 g) oat flour Just put oats in a food processor or blender

2⅓ tbsp (20 g) cornstarch any starch should do the job

¼ cup (20 g) cocoa powder

¼ tsp (2 g) baking soda

½ cup (113 g) unsweetened applesauce

1 egg yolk

¼ cup (60 g) maple syrup

1 tsp (4 g) vanilla

3 tbsp (45 g) chocolate chips

Directions

Preheat your oven to 325°F.

Mix the oat flour, tapioca flour, cocoa powder, and baking soda together until no clumps remain.

In a separate bowl, mix the vanilla, egg yolk, syrup, and apple sauce.

Combine the wet and dry ingredients and add in ⅔ of the chocolate chips.

Spray an 8×8 baking pan with oil and add the mixture. Top with the remaining chocolate chips and bake for 10-12 minutes.

This recipe makes 4 servings.
