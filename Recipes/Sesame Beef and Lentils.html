Sesame Beef and Lentils

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 476 Source: mealprepmanual.com

Ingredients

FOR THE MEAT MIXTURE:

1 lb (454 g) ground beef 85/15

⅔ lb (302 g) green lentils dried

1 tsp (5 ml) sesame oil

5 (20 g) green onions use the whites too

1 tbsp (15 g) ginger minced

1½ tbsp (22 ml) soy sauce

1½ tbsp (22 g) brown sugar honey would work as well

1 tsp (5 g) sriracha

2 tsp (8 g) sesame seeds

FOR THE VEGETABLES:

1 lb (454 g) green beans I use frozen, cut beans

5 oz (142 g) carrots shredded. I buy precut carrots

1 tbsp (15 g) garlic

1 tsp (5 ml) sesame oil

Salt and pepper to taste

FOR THE RICE:

2½ cups (375 g) cooked rice

Directions

FOR THE RICE:

Cook enough rice to yield 2½ cups of cooked rice. 1 cup of dry rice will make between 2-4 cups of cooked rice depending on the type you use.

FOR THE LENTILS:

Rinse your lentils and sift through them to remove any debris.

Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add in the lentils. Cook until they are soft and then drain the water. It will take between 12-15 minutes.

FOR THE MEAT MIXTURE:

Heat a large skillet over medium high heat and cook the beef. Once the beef has browned, drain the fat.

Make some room in the middle of the pan. Heat 1 tsp of sesame oil over medium high heat. Add in the green onions and the ginger and cook for one minute or until fragrant.

Add in the lentils, soy sauce, brown sugar, and sriracha. Stir to incorporate and adjust flavors as you see fit.

FOR THE VEGETABLES:

If you use frozen green beans, thaw them out in the microwave.

Heat a skillet over high heat and add in the sesame oil. Add in the green beans and carrots and allow them to get some color. After 1-2 mintues, add in the garlic and season with salt and pepper to taste. You do not want the garlic to burn.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways. Sprinkle the sesame seeds over the top.
