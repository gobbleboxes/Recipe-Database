Sweet Chili Chicken & Shrimp

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 35 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 507

Ingredients

FOR THE CHICKEN:

2 lbs (908 g) boneless skinless chicken breast

2 tsp (6 g) chili powder

2 tsp (6 g) garlic powder

Salt and pepper to taste

1 Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

5 Tbsp (75 g) Thai sweet chili sauce buy premade

FOR THE RICE:

2 ½ cups (338 g) cooked rice

1 Tbsp (5 g) ginger minced

1 tsp (5 ml) olive oil

Salt to taste

FOR THE SHRIMP:

1 lb (454 g) shrimp

1 Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

1 tsp (5 g) garlic minced

½ tsp (2 g) cumin

½ tsp (2 g) chili powder

½ tsp (2 g) onion powder

¼ tsp (1 g) salt

1 Tbsp (15 ml) lime juice

FOR THE SQUASH:

2 (400 g) zucchini

2 (400 g) yellow squash

1 Tbsp (15 g) garlic minced

1 Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

Directions

FOR THE CHICKEN:

Pound or butterfly your chicken breast so it has an even thickness throughout. Season both sides of the chicken with salt, pepper, 2 tsp chili powder, and 2 tsp garlic powder.

Heat a grill pan or a large skillet over medium high heat and add 1 Tbsp of olive oil.

Cook the chicken until it is no longer pink in the center and the internal temperature reaches 165°F. Allow to rest for 8-10 minutes before slicing. Slice your chicken into strips about ½” thick.

FOR THE SHRIMP:

Mix together the ingredients for the shrimp marinade and stir in the shrimp, set in the fridge to marinate for at least 15 minutes.

Heat a cast iron pan or a grill pan over high heat.

Cook your shrimp for 2 minutes on each side or until fully pink and cooked through.

FOR THE ZUCCHINI:

While the chicken is cooking, slice the zucchini and yellow squash into thin (around 1/8“). Use a mandolin if you have one.

Heat a skillet over medium high heat and add 1 Tbsp of olive oil. Sauté the vegetables and minced garlic until soft. Season with seasoned salt and pepper to taste.

FOR THE RICE:

Cook the rice using your preferred method (rice cooker or saucepan). Be sure you make enough to have 2 ½ cups worth of cooked rice.

In a large bowl, take 2 ½ cups of cooked rice and add the minced ginger, 1 tsp of olive oil, and salt to taste.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. To each of your containers add ½ cup of the ginger rice and divide the chicken, shrimp, and vegetables evenly. Top each piece of chicken with 1 Tbsp of sweet chili sauce.
