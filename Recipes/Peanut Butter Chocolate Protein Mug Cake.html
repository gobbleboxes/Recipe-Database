Peanut Butter Chocolate Protein Mug Cake

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 2 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 351

Ingredients

½ cup (113 g) unsweetened applesauce ⁣

1 scoop (32 g) whey protein ⁣

2 tbsp (10 g) cocoa powder⁣

2 tbsp (10 g) powdered peanut butter ⁣

¼ tsp (1 ml) vanilla ⁣

1 egg⁣

½ tsp (2 g) stevia⁣

1 tsp (5 g) rainbow sprinkles⁣

Directions

Mix everything in a mug and stir until incorporated.

Microwave 2-2 ½ minutes.

Nutrition

Calories: 351kcal | Carbohydrates: 29.4g | Protein: 37.3g | Fat: 9.3g
