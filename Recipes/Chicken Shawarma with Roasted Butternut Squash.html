Chicken Shawarma with Roasted Butternut Squash

Prep Time: 40 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

FOR THE CHICKEN:

2 ½ lbs (1135 g) boneless skinless chicken thighs

3 Tbsp (45 g) garlic minced

¼ cup (60 ml) olive oil

2 Tbsp (30 ml) red wine vinegar

1 Tbsp (8 g) cumin

1 Tbsp (8 g) coriander

1 ½ tsp (3 g) turmeric

1 tsp (2 g) all spice

2 tsp (6 g) salt

1 tsp (2 g) pepper

½ tsp (2 ml) cayenne pepper

FOR THE TAHINI SAUCE:

1 cup (227 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

3 Tbsp (45 ml) tahini

2 Tbsp (30 ml) lemon juice

½ tsp (1.5 g) salt

FOR THE SQUASH:

1 large (1135 g) butternut squash

1 Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

2 tsp (5 g) garlic powder

Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

FOR THE CHICKEN:

Marinate your chicken in a ziplock bag. Using a knife, poke holes into the chicken to allow the marinade to enter the chicken.

To the ziplock bag add the chicken, 3 Tbsp of garlic, ¼ cup olive oil, 2 Tbsp red wine vinegar, 1 Tbsp cumin, 1 Tbsp coriander, 1 ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp all spice, 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, and ½ tsp cayenne pepper. Place in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.

Once the chicken has marinated, heat a grill pan or a skillet over medium high heat. Cook the chicken in batches to prevent crowding the pan. Cook until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F or is no longer pink in the center.

Once the chicken has finished cooking, cut it into strips about ½ inch thick.

FOR THE TAHINI SAUCE:

Mix all of the ingredients together. Season with salt to taste.

FOR THE BUTTERNUT SQUASH:

Preheat your oven to 400°F.

Wash and peel the squash. Cut off the stems and cut the squash in half. Remove the seeds with a spoon. Cut the squash into a large dice, about ½ inch-1 inch pieces.

Spread out the squash on a sheet pan and drizzle with 1 Tbsp of olive oil. Season with 2 tsp of garlic powder, and salt and pepper to taste.

Bake for 10 minutes, then flip and bake another 10 minutes.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways into each of your containers. Store the tahini sauce in a separate container as you do not want to heat this up.
