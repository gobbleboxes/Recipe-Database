Italian Beef and Rice

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: Servings calories: 620 Source:

Ingredients

FOR THE RICE:

3¾ cups (563 g) cooked rice

FOR THE BEEF:

2 lbs (908 g) ground beef (85/15)

6 tbsp (90 g) Italian salad dressing buy your favorite bottled dressing

¾ cup (90 g) sliced pepperoncinis

salt and pepper to taste

FOR THE PEPPERS AND ONIONS

2 small (300 g) sweet onions

2 medium (300 g) red bell peppers

salt and pepper to taste

Directions

FOR THE RICE:

Cook enough rice to have 3¾ cups of cooked rice. 1 cup of dry rice will yield about 2-3 cups of cooked rice depending on what kind you use.

FOR THE BEEF:

In a large skillet over medium high heat, add the ground beef and brown it. Wash and cut your vegetables while the beef is cooking. To save on using oil, we are going to use the grease released by the beef to cook the vegetables.

Once the beef has browned, drain away the fat and reserve for the vegetables.

Mix in 6 tbsp of Italian dressing and ¾ cup of sliced pepperocinis. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

FOR THE PEPPERS AND ONIONS

Wash and cut your peppers and onions into thin slices. Try and cut the peppers to roughly ⅛" in thickness.

In a large skillet over medium high heat, add in some of the grease from the ground beef. FYI, you don't have to wait until the beef has finished cooking to take this from the pan. You can spoon out the grease as it is produced so that you can cook the beef and vegetables simultaneously to save time.

Add in the peppers and onions and salt lightly. Cook the peppers and onions for 10-15 minutes until the onions have deeply caramelized and the peppers are very soft. Make sure you stir frequently to ensure they don't burn.

Adding a small amount of water to the pan can help to cook these faster through steam.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Each dish gets ¾ cup of cooked rice and you can divide your remaining ingredients evenly between your 5 containers.
