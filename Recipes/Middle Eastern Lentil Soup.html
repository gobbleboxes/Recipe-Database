Middle Eastern Lentil Soup

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 427

Ingredients

FOR THE SOUP:

1 medium (200 g) onion⁣

1 cup (150 g) carrots chopped

1 tbsp (15 g) olive oil⁣

2 tsp (6 g) cumin⁣

½ tsp (2 g) turmeric ⁣

1 ½ cups (350 g) red lentils ⁣

2 cups (480 ml) water⁣

4 cups (960 ml) vegetable broth ⁣

1 tbsp (15 g) garlic ⁣

3 tbsp (45 ml) lemon juice ⁣

FOR THE YOGURT:

½ cup (100 g) plain non fat Greek yogurt

1 tsp (5 g) garlic ⁣ minced

(1 ml) Splash of lemon juice⁣

Water to thin ⁣

1 (60 g) Roma tomato ⁣

Directions

FOR THE SOUP:

Cut the onion and carrot into a small dice. Mince the garlic. ⁣

Add 1 tbsp of oil to a large stock pot and sauté onion, carrot, and garlic for 3-5 minutes. ⁣

Add in the turmeric and cumin. Stir. ⁣

Add in the lentils, water, and broth. Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce heat to low to simmer for 15 minutes. ⁣

Stir in lemon juice and season with salt and pepper to taste. ⁣

FOR THE YOGURT:

Mix a splash of lemon and garlic into the yogurt. Top the soup with yogurt and tomato. ⁣
