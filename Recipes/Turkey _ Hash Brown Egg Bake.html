Turkey & Hash Brown Egg Bake

Breakfast

Prep Time: 8 minutes Cook Time: 35 minutes Servings: Servings: calories: 404 Source: mealprepmanual.

Ingredients

1 lb (454 g) ground turkey (93/7)

10 (10) eggs

¼ cup (60 ml) skim milk

2 cups (168 g) shredded hash browns

½ (100 g) sweet onion

1 (150 g) poblano pepper

1 (150 g) red pepper

½ cup (56 g) shredded cheese

1 Tbsp (15 ml) olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

2 ½ cups (75 g) spinach (optional)

Cooking spray

Directions

Preheat your oven to 375°F.

Wash your peppers and chop the peppers and onion into a small dice.

Heat 1 Tbsp of olive oil over medium high heat in a large skillet. Add in the peppers and onions and cook for 2-3 minutes. If you wish, add 2 ½ cup of raw spinach to the pan and wilt.

Add in the ground turkey and brown.

While the turkey is cooking, beat 10 eggs and ¼ cup of milk together. Stir in 2 cups of shredded hash browns, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, and ½ cup of shredded cheese.

Once the turkey has finished cooking, mix it into the egg/hash brown mixture.

Lightly spray a 13”x9” baking dish with cooking spray and pour in the egg mixture. Bake for 20-22 minutes or until the center has set.

PLATING

This recipe makes 5 servings. Cut the Egg Bake into 5 equal servings.
